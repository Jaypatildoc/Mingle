About Us

Meet the India-based advertising agency that is bringing brands to life with vibrant story-telling and design.

Over the last decade, MINGLE has created design strategies for clients across national and international industries, most prominently institute and Schools, Architecture & construction firm, dental care, hearing solutions, hotels, retail brands and sports among others.

Vision

Making Mingle a well-known brand through creative marketing and brand strategy and helping businesses and start-ups grow. .

Mission

To provide the best value at an affordable cost.

Our Services

Digital Marketing

This encompasses a range of services including website development, search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), social media marketing, email marketing, content marketing, and online advertising (such as display ads, video ads, and native advertising).

Branding and Identity

Agencies help clients develop or refine their brand identity, including logo design, brand messaging, brand positioning, and brand guidelines to ensure consistency across all marketing channels.

3D Designing and CGI Ads

3D design consists of 3D models using various software for projects like 3D preparation or research articles. Whereas we provide a trending and new concept of 3D CGI ads to establish your brand awareness.

Strategic Planning

Agencies help clients define their marketing objectives, identify target audiences, and develop comprehensive strategies to achieve their goals.

Market Research

Agencies conduct market research to gather insights into consumer behavior, market trends, and competitive landscape, helping clients make informed decisions about their marketing efforts.

Creative Services

This includes the development of advertising concepts, copywriting, graphic design, and multimedia production for various platforms such as print, digital, broadcast, and outdoor advertising.
